ThreeFold

ThreeFold is an ambitious venture by OurWorld aimed at revolutionizing digital infrastructure and internet accessibility worldwide. It focuses on creating a decentralized and sustainable internet that is more private, secure, and efficient.

ThreeFold aims to build a peer-to-peer internet grid that is owned and operated by its users, eliminating the need for centralized data centers.
Decentralized Internet
By leveraging renewable energy and efficient hardware, ThreeFold strives to minimize the environmental impact of digital infrastructure.
Sustainability
Providing universal access to the internet, especially in underserved regions, through its decentralized grid.
Accessibility
Enhancing privacy and security by decentralizing data storage and processing, reducing vulnerabilities to cyber threats.
Security

Empowering through Digital Sovereignty

OurWorld is committed to supporting ThreeFold's mission to democratize internet access and empower individuals and communities globally through innovative digital infrastructure solutions.
